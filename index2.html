Web Application Programming and Hacking

Instructor: Dr. Phu Phung

Course Overview

The foundational ideas of web application development are covered in this course, along with front-end technologies and back-end technologies. Through hands-on exercises, it will also explore web application attacks and defenses. Students will also study programming concepts to protect against vulnerabilities and attacks.
